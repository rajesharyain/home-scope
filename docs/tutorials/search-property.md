# How to Search a Property

> Get neighbourhood insights for any address in seconds.

---

## Step 1: Open the App

![Home Screen](../../automation/screenshots/search-property/01-open_app.png)

Open HomeScope. You'll see the main search screen with the headline "Move smarter. Invest wiser." and a search field below it.

## Step 2: Enter an Address

![Search Input](../../automation/screenshots/search-property/02-search_field.png)

Tap the search field and type a property address, neighbourhood name, or city. HomeScope supports addresses across Portugal.

## Step 3: Choose a Suggestion

![Suggestions](../../automation/screenshots/search-property/03-suggestions.png)

As you type, address suggestions appear below the search field. Tap one to fill the search box automatically.

## Step 4: Tap Get Insights

![Get Insights Button](../../automation/screenshots/search-property/04-get_insights.png)

Tap the **Get Insights** button to begin the analysis. HomeScope will score the neighbourhood across 7 dimensions.

## Step 5: View the Results

![Results Overview](../../automation/screenshots/search-property/05-results_overview.png)

The Neighbourhood Report opens showing an overall score out of 100, a breakdown by category, an AI-generated summary, and a historical timeline.

### What the score covers

| Dimension | What it measures |
|-----------|-----------------|
| 🚇 Transport | Proximity to metro, bus, and rail |
| 🎓 Education | Schools, universities, libraries |
| 🏥 Health | Hospitals, clinics, pharmacies |
| 🛡 Safety | Crime data and emergency services |
| 🛍 Lifestyle | Restaurants, cafes, shops |
| 🌳 Nature | Parks, green spaces, recreation |
| 💼 Investment | Property trends and market data |

---

*Tip: Tap any category card to see a detailed breakdown.*
