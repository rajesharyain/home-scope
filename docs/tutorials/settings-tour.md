# How to Use Settings & Profile

> Personalise HomeScope so it works the way you do — from your search radius to AI-powered summaries.

---

## Step 1: Open the You Tab

![You Tab](../../automation/screenshots/settings-tour/01-you_tab.png)

Tap the **You** icon in the bottom navigation bar. The screen is divided into two sections: your profile and your preferences. Everything you set here is saved locally on your device.

## Step 2: Select Your Profile Type

![Profile Selection](../../automation/screenshots/settings-tour/02-profile_selection.png)

HomeScope tailors its recommendations to who you are. Tap your profile to choose from:

- **First-time Buyer** — prioritises affordability and transport links
- **Investor** — emphasises rental yield, price trends, and market liquidity
- **Family** — weights education, safety, and green space more heavily
- **Remote Worker** — focuses on connectivity, cafes, and quality of life

Your current selection is highlighted in blue. The rest of the app updates immediately to reflect your chosen perspective.

## Step 3: Set Your Country

![Country Selector](../../automation/screenshots/settings-tour/03-country_selector.png)

Tap **Country** to select your target market. HomeScope currently supports **Portugal** with detailed neighbourhood data for Lisbon, Porto, Braga, Coimbra, and the Algarve. Additional countries will be added in future updates.

## Step 4: Adjust the Search Radius

![Search Radius](../../automation/screenshots/settings-tour/04-search_radius.png)

The **Search Radius** slider controls how far from your searched address HomeScope looks when calculating category scores. Drag the slider left for a tighter, hyperlocal analysis or right for a broader neighbourhood view. The default is 500 metres.

## Step 5: Choose Your Appearance

![Appearance Settings](../../automation/screenshots/settings-tour/05-appearance.png)

Tap **Appearance** to switch between **Light**, **Dark**, and **System** themes. The System option automatically follows your device's display setting. HomeScope's dark theme uses a deep navy background that is easy on the eyes in low-light conditions.

## Step 6: Manage AI Features

![AI Features Toggle](../../automation/screenshots/settings-tour/06-ai_features.png)

The **AI Features** section controls the OpenAI-powered elements of the app. Toggle options include:

- **AI Summary** — A plain-English overview of the neighbourhood generated by GPT-4.
- **Investment Commentary** — Context-aware market observations for your profile type.
- **Trend Explanations** — Sentence-level explanations of score changes over time.

Turning off AI features reduces API usage and speeds up results on slow connections.

## Step 7: Access the In-App Tutorial

![In-App Tutorial](../../automation/screenshots/settings-tour/07-in_app_tutorial.png)

Scroll to the bottom of the You tab and tap **How to Use HomeScope** to open the in-app tutorial carousel. It walks through the core features with annotated screenshots — the same content covered in this guide, available offline at any time.

---

### Quick reference

| Setting | Where to find it | Effect |
|---------|-----------------|--------|
| Profile | You → Profile | Reweights category scores for your use case |
| Country | You → Country | Selects the property market dataset |
| Search Radius | You → Search Radius | Controls the geographic scope of scoring |
| Appearance | You → Appearance | Switches Light / Dark / System theme |
| AI Summary | You → AI Features | Enables GPT-4 neighbourhood overview |
| Investment Commentary | You → AI Features | Adds market context for investors |
| Trend Explanations | You → AI Features | Explains score changes over time |
| Tutorial | You → How to Use HomeScope | Opens the in-app feature walkthrough |

---

*Tip: You do not need an account to use HomeScope. All preferences are stored on-device and sync if iCloud backup is enabled.*
