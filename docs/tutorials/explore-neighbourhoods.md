# How to Explore Neighbourhoods

> Discover the neighbourhoods that match your lifestyle — without typing a single address.

---

## Step 1: Open the Explore Tab

![Explore Tab](../../automation/screenshots/explore-neighbourhoods/01-explore_tab.png)

From the bottom navigation bar, tap **Explore**. The tab shows a curated grid of neighbourhoods across Portugal, each with a thumbnail, name, city, and overall score.

## Step 2: Browse the Neighbourhood Grid

![Neighbourhood Grid](../../automation/screenshots/explore-neighbourhoods/02-neighbourhood_grid.png)

Scroll down to browse all available neighbourhoods. Each card shows a cover image, the neighbourhood name, the city it belongs to, and a coloured score badge. Higher scores indicate more livable, well-connected areas.

## Step 3: Filter by Category

![Category Filter](../../automation/screenshots/explore-neighbourhoods/03-category_filter.png)

Tap the filter chips at the top of the Explore screen to narrow results by what matters to you. Available filters include **Transport**, **Education**, **Health**, **Safety**, **Lifestyle**, **Nature**, and **Investment**. Active filters are highlighted in blue.

## Step 4: Apply Multiple Filters

![Multiple Filters Active](../../automation/screenshots/explore-neighbourhoods/04-multi_filter.png)

You can tap more than one filter at the same time. The grid updates instantly to show only neighbourhoods that score well across all selected dimensions. Tap a filter chip again to remove it.

## Step 5: Tap a Neighbourhood Card

![Neighbourhood Card Tap](../../automation/screenshots/explore-neighbourhoods/05-card_tap.png)

Tap any neighbourhood card to open its full Neighbourhood Report. You'll see the same detailed breakdown as a search result — overall score, category scores, AI summary, and timeline.

## Step 6: Read the Neighbourhood Report

![Neighbourhood Report](../../automation/screenshots/explore-neighbourhoods/06-neighbourhood_report.png)

The report shows everything HomeScope knows about this area. Scroll through the category cards for individual scores, read the AI-generated summary for a plain-English overview, and check the historical timeline to see how the neighbourhood has changed over time.

### Score categories at a glance

| Category | High score means… |
|----------|-------------------|
| 🚇 Transport | Excellent metro, bus, and rail connections |
| 🎓 Education | Many schools, universities, and libraries nearby |
| 🏥 Health | Close access to hospitals, clinics, and pharmacies |
| 🛡 Safety | Low crime indicators and good emergency coverage |
| 🛍 Lifestyle | Rich mix of restaurants, cafes, shops, and culture |
| 🌳 Nature | Ample parks, green corridors, and recreational space |
| 💼 Investment | Strong property trends and rental yield potential |

---

*Tip: Save a neighbourhood to your favourites by tapping the bookmark icon on the report screen.*
