Our Story

I built this because my family needed it

A few years ago, we were searching for our first family home in Portugal. We weren't just looking for the right house — we needed the right neighbourhood.

"Will my children be able to walk to school? What if there's an emergency — how far is the nearest hospital? Is public transport good enough that we don't need a second car? Will there be parks nearby for weekends?"

We spent weeks opening Google Maps tab after tab, calculating walking distances one by one, reading local forums, cross-referencing apps. It was exhausting — and we still weren't confident we had the complete picture.

So I built HomeScope. Now I can paste any address and within seconds see every school, hospital, transit stop, and park within walking distance — with an AI-generated summary and a single score out of 100.

A decision that used to take our family three weeks now takes thirty seconds. We want every family to have that clarity.
